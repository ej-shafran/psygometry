The scores above are calculated based on the "raw" and "uniform" scores. These can be useful to understand how many questions were incorrectly answered, and in which sections.

Raw scores are simply the amount of answers you answered correctly in that specific domain, over all the relevant sections. Uniform scores are that raw score normalized to a scale between 50 and 150.
